Creaste an account to play at local game stores

You first have to download and app called Bandai TCG Plus. After the app is downloaded, you will go to the others tab at the bottom and there will be a place to sign up/in. You will follow a couple different steps and once finished your account it ready.

To signup for a local event at any local game store you have to go to the search event tab. The easiest way to look for a store is to hit the event detail search and type in the name of the store. If there are multiple with that name, there is the adress right in under the name. Check that to make sure you have the right store.
